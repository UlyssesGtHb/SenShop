Bundle Description

The VCT x SEN Capsule is a collection of cosmetics in VALORANT as part of VCT Team Capsules showcasing the VCT Americas Team Sentinels. Its contents were available in the Esports Store whenever the VCT season is available. Items within the set could not be purchased individually; players had to buy each of the complete capsule sets to unlock any of its contents. A portion of net proceeds from each capsule were shared to the respective VCT partner teams in each season.

FAQs

What is included in the Bundle/Capsule?

As Mentioned in the 'Key Features' tab, it includes the classic skin itself, player card, gun buddy, and a spray.

When Can you Snag These?

Throughout the 2025 VCT Season, you can purchase Capsules in VALORANT through the brand new in-game Esports Store - just go to the Store page and click on the “Esports” tab. Capsules will go live in most regions alongside the release of the current Patch.

Why Team Capsules?

It goes beyond being a cool skin that is worthy of being in your collection (although that's still a part of it). It's about creating a vibrant ecosystem where fans and teams thrive together.

Does it improve accuracy?

Well, yes and no! Yes, in a way that boosts your confidence and show off your support for the organization. And No, because Riot Games will not allow any form of cheating as it breaks the competitive integrity.
